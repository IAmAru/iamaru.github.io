GAME: Shadows of the Damned: Hella Remastered
PLATFORM: PlayStation 5
STARTED ON: 1/20
BEATEN ON: 1/20
TOTAL PLAYTIME: 10 hours

Super fun!
Only took a day over the weekend to knock this one out. I'm really glad I picked it up from VGP recently. I skipped on it during its initial LRG print.
I don't know if I want to call it short... You know, 10 hours for one casual run is a good chunk of game. It reminded me a lot of Resident Evil 4. In fact, I joked about this with a friend and he said he thought the exact same thing.
The gameplay, the puzzle-solving, the whole chasing a blonde girl into a terrifying place bit... Wonder if there's any overlap there.
Even still, it manages to have enough unique quirks to set itself apart, including really over the top absurd humor and charming one liners such as "taste my big boner", or the great exchange of "hard men like us don't mix", with the reply of "was that a come on?". I'm definitely a fan. Just random ass humor undermining the setting. It's one of those creative games from a collaboration Suda51 and Shinji Mikami (probably where the Resi4 comes from). Soundtrack by Akira Yamaoka.
