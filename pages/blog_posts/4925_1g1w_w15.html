GAME: Higurashi When They Cry: Chapter 5 - Meakashi
PLATFORM: PC (Steam Deck)
STARTED ON: 3/15
BEATEN ON: 3/20
TOTAL PLAYTIME: 23.6 Hours

This is the third Higurashi chapter I've finished this year. It's funny looking back. It really feels as if I've been sucked into the story, starting from Tatarigoroshi (Ch3).
As a couple days back, I've actually already finished Chapter 6, Tsumihoroboshi, so please look forward to the Week 18 post, lol.
I don't think I could forgive myself if I talked about this too much, and spoiled someone who could possibly get into Higurashi, as slim of a possibility as that is, with my heavily limited audience (thank you very much for reading my posts!).

As such, I'm not going to talk about my thoughts, or the pieces I've put together, or how right or otherwise I am about the mysteries of Hinamizawa. I've shared a lot of thoughts with my friends who have actually finished, so at least they're documented... somewhere.
When I finished Meakashi, I figured I was starting to put most of the pieces together. It's a big puzzle. It's a fun game. The actual visual novel part may not be too much of a game, but the thinking part definitely is.
It's a social game. Sure, the "gameplay", strictly speaking, is just pressing the A button. Occasionally the left bumper if I want to set it to Auto Mode. The real fun starts with theorizing- with talking to other people experiencing the novel alongside you, or people who already know the answers.
