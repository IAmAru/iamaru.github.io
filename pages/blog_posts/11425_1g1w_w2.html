GAME: Zone of the Enders (HD Collection)
PLATFORM: PlayStation 3

A friend of mine recommended this game a lot, since he got a PS2 and used totally legal means to play it, so I figured I'd give it a shot as well.

It was pretty fun. Giant robots in simple objective-based missions, with the interesting mechanic of city damage factoring into mission ranking or score.
Picked it up a little while back from a retro game shop that's since closed down around April of last year. It was my last purchase there actually :(
Kojima game, so it's decently... creative. What I thought was awesome was that the giant robots actually have the same mecha designer as Metal Gear, Yoji Shinkawa.
ADA (AI companion character) was really annoying to listen to sometimes. It felt like listening to a Twitch chat TTS saying the same lines over and over, as she narrates actions you take, building destruction, etc.
But... I already know I entered combat! I pressed the button to do it! I know I damaged a building accidentally! It blew up dramatically, so I can tell! Shut up!

Zone of the Enders was actually a decently short game. Maybe a 6-7 hour game, if you wanna do everything. It's pretty enjoyable!
